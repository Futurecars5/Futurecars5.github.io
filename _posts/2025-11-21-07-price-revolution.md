By **2030** the world’s cheapest new car will be electric — **under $10,000**.  
By **2045** owning a car will be 5–10× more expensive than using a robotaxi.

This is already happening.

![Tesla Cybercab $0.20/mile target](/images/14.jpg)

### Why personal ownership dies
- Average car sits idle 96 % of time → robotaxi used 60–70 %
- One robotaxi replaces 8–12 private cars
- No insurance, no parking, no maintenance for passenger
- China already has robotaxi fleets cheaper than buses (2024)

In 2045 only enthusiasts and rural areas will own cars.  
Everyone else will press a button and pay 7 cents per km.
